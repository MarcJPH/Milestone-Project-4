Nutrition

Selected Pre & Post Workout foods by our experts to make the most of your exercise

    Pre Workout

  • 1/4 Avacado & Whole Grain Toast

    If your workout starts within 2-3 hours, have some whole grain toast topped with avacado and maybe add a cup of fruit to ensure you have all the energy you need.

  • Egg Omelette & Whole-Grain Toast

    An egg omelette with whole grain toast will provide the energy you require for your workout in 2 hours.

  • Protein Smoothie

    If your workout is less than 2 hours away, have a delicious protein smoothie made with milk, some protein powder, banana and mixed berries. You will have exactly the energy you need to hit your workout hard.

    Post Workout

  • Salmon & Sweet Potato

    After a tough but enjoyable workout, why not try some salmon and sweet potato that will provide your body with the valuable nutrients to help you keep progressing on your fitness journey.

  • Grilled Chicken & Roasted Veg

    The grilled chicken and roasted vegetables dish will remove that post workout hunger and give you the protein your muscles require.

  • Kale salad with berries and pecans

    An excellent dish that will not only provide you all the nutrients after your workout but also tastes excellent.
